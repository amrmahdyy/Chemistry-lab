At Multiphase Reactors Engineering and Application Laboratory (mReal), we are involved in research and technological development for advancing multiphase and multiscale processes. Our sophisticated measurement techniques and expertise benefit different sectors, ranging from petroleum, petrochemical, gas and renewable energy, to chemical, biochemical, waste treatment, environmental, and well, Multiphase Reaction Engineering Laboratory. We collaborate with other universities and with industries to advance the field of engineering. Our motto here is simple. We believe that for every process, laboratory or commercial scale, it is as important to lay a strong foundation of fundamental principles, as it is to reinforce the process with new and advanced technology.
